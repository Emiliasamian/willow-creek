Entering House

As you enter your house, you heard some footsteps in your room. You carefully approach your room and see the back of a woman going through your stuff. You threaten to call the police on her.

She slowly turn around and to your surprise, it's mum. She ran towards you and gave you a hug. Tears streaming down her cheeks.

Mum: "Oh I miss you so much. But we need to leave here quickly. Before your dad finds us. I promise to explain everything but we got to go now."
